Amenities

Dining

  • Breakfast Menu
  • Lunch Menu
  • Dinner Menu

Nutritious meals that are cooked on-site, are served in our atrium dining room. There are two selections to choose from at lunch and dinner. Our full service package includes three meals a day. Rental only residents have the ability to buy meals on those days that they want someone else to cook for them.

A private dining room is available for apartment residents to book should they wish to host a special occasion meal and need a little more space. The dining room table comfortably sits eight.
