Volunteer

volunteer

We are always looking for healthcare professionals who share in our passion for providing quality care and superior levels of service to our residents! Imagine having the opportunity to enhance our resident’s lives…all within a caring, compassionate environment! Quality care can only be achieved though quality people!

Activation Programming

assist the Activity Staff in providing recreational activity programs for residents

Out-Trips

assist the staff with outings into the Community, either with staff on a bus tour or with staff on a walking/ wheelchair tour

Dining Room Assistant

assist in providing a pleasurable dining experience – a special orientation is required and provided

Spiritual Programming

assist in meeting the spiritual needs of the Residents through music, worship or reflective opportunities

Musician

volunteers of all types are used for storytelling, singers and musical entertainment

Social Visitor

assist in offering social interaction and one to one visiting with Residents

Gardening

assist in the maintenance and watering of the indoor and outdoor plants

Pet Therapy

assist in providing pet therapy to the Residents. All pets to have training and current vaccinations and papers

Manicures

assist in providing manicures to the Residents

Pastoral Visits

provide companionship and communication based on meeting the spiritual needs of the Residents

Portering

assisting with transporting Residents to activities, meals and hairdressing

Other Opportunities

there are other opportunities which exist for those volunteers who share special talents or interests that suit the Home and the Residents needs
