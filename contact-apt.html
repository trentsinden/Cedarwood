F.A.Q's

How do I know if moving to Cedarwood Village Apartments will be the right move for me?
Cedarwood Village Apartments are independent living facilities. If you require round-the-clock help with eating, dressing and using the bathroom, or require regular medical assistance, other housing options such as assisted living facilities or long term care facilities may be a better fit.

Independent living facilities are about making your life easier. If you are finding that your current home is too much for you to take care of or is difficult to access, we can take away these burdens. Additionally the onsite amenities and services available to you, such as weekly shopping trips, in house laundry amenities, social activities and meal services can make daily activities more manageable.
Are nursing services available in the apartments?
Cedarwood Village Apartments are independent living facilities. Nursing services are not provided within the apartments other than those arranged by the apartment resident through outside sources.
Are the units furnished?
Cedarwood Village apartments include an apartment sized fridge and stove. As the Apartment resident you will provide all other furniture allowing you to personalize your apartment.
