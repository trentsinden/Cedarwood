About

Our Commitment

We are committed to a tradition of respect for your dignity and privacy. Our pledge is to continually strive to enrich your lifestyle and provide you with a level of independence you might not have at home.

Location

Located in Simcoe Ontario, Cedarwood Village operates a 40 apartment complex for seniors. The facility is situated on a 6.5 acre parcel of land located on highway 3, just a few minutes drive from the local hospital, various local restaurants and shopping.

Rooms

Cedarwood Village includes 40 independent living senior apartments with controlled entry for security. Various design layouts are available including one or two bedroom units ranging in size from 614 square feet to 1025 square feet.

Amenities

Attractive physical surroundings complimented by a professional standard of service are a hallmark of Cedarwood Village Retirement Apartments. We have an on-site Activity Coordinator who plans a variety of activities and outings throughout the month.

Family owned and operated for over 30 years, Cedarwood prides itself on providing clean facilities and friendly quality care to the residents that call our facility home.
