About

Our Vision

Cedarwood Village Long Term Care is a 90 bed facility licensed by the Ministry of Long Term Care. Our Vision: To appreciate the hearts and meaning of all people.

Visits

The accessible outside courtyard and gazebo area is a popular visiting space complete with perennial flower gardens.

Rooms

Cedarwood is an CARF accredited facility offering a mixture of private, semi private and ward accommodations. Our home offers spacious lounge areas for activities arranged by our qualified activity staff or for visiting with family members and friends.

Services

Nursing services are provided by qualified nursing staff who genuinely care for the well being of all of our residents.
